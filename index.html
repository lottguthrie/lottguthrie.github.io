About Me

I am 32 years old, and have been married for approximately 12 years. My wife, Emily, and I have a 2 year old daughter, Sophia, and currently live in Morgans Point Resort, which is located outside of Temple, TX on Belton Lake. I am a combat veteran of both the Iraq and Afghanistan wars, and honorably served in the Army from 2007-2014. I then became a Field Training Officer and Detective with the Killeen Police Department, located in Killeen, TX, approximately 45 north of Austin, TX. I became interested in full stack web developement while serving as a police officer. What peaked my interest was the lack of good programs for law enforcement purposes. I wanted to learn how to create and improve the current programs utilized by members of public safety.
